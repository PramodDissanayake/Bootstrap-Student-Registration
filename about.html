About Us

Our Mission

We aim to provide quality education in the field of information technology, empowering students with the skills and knowledge to succeed in their careers.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in the region, recognized for excellence in education and innovation in technology.

Our Values

At Ceylon Information Technical Institute (CIT), we are committed to:

  • Providing accessible and affordable education
  • Fostering a culture of continuous learning and growth
  • Promoting diversity, inclusivity, and respect for all individuals
  • Encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
